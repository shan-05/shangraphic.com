We would like to give you a great Service.Most people are fairly confident in their definition of graphic design, but they end up pigeonholing graphic designers into a very narrow scope simply because they have a limited idea of what designers actually do. Do they create logos for businesses? Do they edit images in Photoshop? Do they design graphics for digital advertising? Yes, but these are only elements of a larger picture.Design is everything from the physical to the digital to everywhere in between. There is a lot more to graphic design than a stunning website or stand-out business cards. These are just smaller elements of larger categories. To fully understand the world of graphic design, we must break it down into 11 parts. They are- 1. Branding/Visual Identity 2. Advertising & Marketing Design 3. Digital Design 4. Product Design 4. Editorial/Publishing 5. Packaging 7. Lettering/Typeface Design 8. Environmental Design 9. Set Prop Design 10. Human-Centered Design 11. Design for Good .
